✦ 6 YEARS OF US ✦

A 2D platformer love story — browser, Phaser.js

1

💻 Quarantine Quest

Two glowing screens in the dark. Chat bubbles float between them. Hearts appear as the messages keep coming. No gameplay — pure story intro.
📍 2020 · COVID · Online
CUTSCENE
2

✈️ First Flight

Play as Jez — run through the airport, jump over luggage carts, collect boarding passes. Mae is waiting at the arrivals gate holding a sign. Reach her to complete the level.
📍 Mindanao → Manila · Airport
PLAYABLE
3

🌲 The Baguio Quest

Both characters run together through pine forests and the iconic Burnham Park. Collect strawberries and flowers. First time as a duo — the level is brighter and warmer than the rest.
📍 Baguio City · Mountains
PLAYABLE
4

🗺️ Long Distance

A map of the Philippines. Two blinking dots — one in Manila, one in Mindanao. Love letters and emoji float slowly between them. A countdown ticker shows the days apart.
📍 Manila ↔ Mindanao · LDR
CUTSCENE
5

💼 The Grind

Side-scrolling auto-runner. Jez and Mae each on their own side of the screen, collecting coins and dodging bills/stress clouds. They're working towards the same goal. Coins fill a shared "Manila Fund" bar at the top.
📍 Working · Saving · Grinding
PLAYABLE
6

🏠 Manila, Finally

City level — apartment hunting (jump to reach the right unit!), eating out at food stalls, going on dates. Both characters run together through the city. Collect keys, ramen bowls, and shared memories.
📍 Manila · Apartment · Dates · Everyday quests
PLAYABLE
7

✨ A Blink of an Eye

Montage cutscene. Six memory "frames" flash by — one per year. A heart counter ticks up: 1… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6. The screen fills with sparkles. "And just like that…"
📍 6 Years · Present Day
